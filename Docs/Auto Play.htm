Auto Play

 

If the Windows Autoplay feature is enabled on your computer, the launch screen will begin when you insert the Ultimate Spider-Man™ CD one into your CD-ROM drive.  Press INSTALL and follow the on-screen instructions to continue the installation. 

 

*Note: Ultimate Spider-Man™ CD one functions as the CD key.  You must have CD one in your CD-ROM drive to play the game.

If the Autoplay feature is disabled, you can begin the installation by doing the following:
a) Go to the Start Menu and select Run.
b) Type D:\SETUP.EXE (assuming D is your CD-ROM drive letter) and press
Enter.

-or-

a) Double-click on My Computer
b) Double-Click your CD-ROM drive icon.
c) Double-click on SETUP.EXE.
